Punjab

Punjabi cuisine is known for its rich, hearty flavors. Signature dishes include butter chicken, sarson da saag (mustard greens), and makki di roti (cornmeal flatbread). The cuisine is characterized by dairy (like paneer and ghee), tandoori cooking, and robust spices.

Maharashtra

Maharashtra cuisine is diverse, raging from coastal seafood to to spiced vegetarian dishes. Popular food include puran poli [sweet flatbread] , vada pav [spicy potato fritter in a bun], and fish curries from coastal areas like Kolhapur.
