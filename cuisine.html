canadian

Canadian cuisine combines Indigenous traditions with British and French influences. Popular foods include poutine, maple syrup dishes, and butter tarts. Seafood is important in coastal regions, while meat and potato dishes are common in colder areas. Canada is especially known for producing high-quality maple syrup.
